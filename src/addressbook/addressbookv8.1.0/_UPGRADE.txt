Basic Upgrade
==============
0. Backup at least the "/config" directory or all files.

1. Unzip the files and place them on your webserver.

2. Restore the old "config.php".

Advanced Upgrade
================
3. Backup your database, at least the "addressbook" + "user" tables.

4. Apply the database patches fromm "./upgrades".

5. Have a look at "_INSTALL.txt" for new features.
